The Happy Ending

Not every show needs a happy ending --- but for Blue and Mayko the ending is crucial, and sometimes it takes knowing the ending to have the courage to embark on a 50 episode cryfest.

A key function of this site is to track information on dramas' endings— recording whether a given drama has a happy ending (HE) or not.
This mission is inspired by much pain in our past.
